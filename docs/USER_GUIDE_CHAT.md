## â“ FAQs

**Q: How do I know if I have unread messages?**
A: Look for red badges with numbers on conversation cards. The number shows how many unread messages you have.

**Q: Can I message someone before matching?**
A: No, you need to create a match first by clicking "Connect & Chat" on their match card.

**Q: What if I accidentally click "Connect & Chat" twice?**
A: Don't worry! The system detects duplicate matches and just opens your existing chat.

**Q: How do I see someone's profile?**
A: Click "View Profile" in the chat header, or click their profile picture.

**Q: Are my messages saved?**
A: Yes! All message history is saved and synced across devices.

**Q: Can I use chat on mobile?**
A: Yes! The chat works on any device with a web browser.

**Q: How do I know if someone read my message?**
A: TalkJS shows read receipts when messages are seen.

**Q: Can I send images?**
A: Yes! TalkJS supports images, links, and rich content.
